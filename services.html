Care & Convenience

Pharmacy Solutions

We provide everything you expect from a modern pharmacy, plus the customized care and attention you can only get from a local partner.

Prescription Delivery

We deliver your prescriptions directly to your home or office. Same-day delivery is free within Stockton, saving you time and travel. Call us to confirm your delivery schedule.

Easy Refills

Skip the wait times. Request refills online anytime using our simple Refill RX form, or call us. We process requests promptly so your medications are ready when you need them.

Long-Term Care (LTC)

We specialize in serving group homes, assisted living, and skilled nursing facilities. We offer custom compliance packaging (multi-dose blister packs), medication administration records (MARs), and consulting.

Medication Counseling

Have questions about your prescriptions? Our clinical team provides patient counseling. We explain how your medications work, potential side effects, and how to maximize their benefits.

Insurance Coordination

Navigating insurance can be difficult. We work directly with insurance providers, Medicare, and Medi-Cal to resolve coverage issues, coordinate prior authorizations, and minimize co-pays.

Medication Synchronization

Avoid making multiple trips to the pharmacy. Our Med Sync program aligns your prescriptions so that they are all filled on the same day each month, simplifying your schedule.

Looking for something else?

We offer a wide range of customized services to meet your specific health needs. Contact our team today to see how we can assist you with special orders, vaccinations, or other pharmacy solutions.
