Our Story

Serving Stockton with Pride

Stockton Pharmacy is operated by BJRX Pharmacy LTC, LLC, a dedicated healthcare organization focused on providing premier pharmacy services. We fill the gap between large, impersonal pharmacy networks and the custom care our community deserves.

We provide comprehensive retail services to individuals and specialized long-term care (LTC) solutions to facilities and group homes. Our focus is always on efficiency, accuracy, and genuine human connection.
